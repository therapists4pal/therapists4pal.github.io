Client Agreement

I agree that Therapists 4 Palestine (T4P) is not responsible for vetting credibility, competency, or safety of therapists listed, and that the presence of a therapist on this directory does not mean an endorsement of this therapist by T4P.

I agree that it's my responsibility to vet any potential therapists I find on this directory (e.g., through their licensing bodies, professional associations, web search, word of mouth, and interviewing during an initial consultation call).

I understand that a therapist's appearance on this directory reflects that this therapist has endorsed T4P's Mission & Values.

I understand that this directory may list both practitioners who are licensed by a Canadian licensing body as well as practitioners who are not licensed by any Canadian licensing body.

Therapist Agreement

I embrace and commit to Therapists 4 Palestine's Mission & Values as given on this website.

I commit to abide by any laws governing the practice of psychotherapy or counselling in the province(s) where I practice (e.g., the Psychotherapist Act, 2007 in Ontario).

I commit to abide by the professional standards issued by any licensing body with which I am registered (e.g., in Ontario the CRPO, CNO, OCSWSSW, COTO, CPSO, CPBAO).

I commit to uphold my professional Code of Ethics.

I understand that T4P is not a source of professional accountability to me, and that it is my responsibility to engage in ongoing professional development (e.g., consultation, supervision, training) in order to cultivate a safe and effective use of self with clients who may seek my services.

I understand that if any information comes to T4P's attention that raises concerns about my credibility or my ability to practice safely, T4P reserves the right and discretion to remove me from this directory, and, if necessary, to share this information with my licensing and/or professional body.
